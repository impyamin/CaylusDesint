\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
\begin{DoxyVerb}A set per player of all 12 player buildings (cards of the color of the player): small farm, small sawmill,
small quarry, peddler, market, lawyer, large farm, large sawmill, large quarry, gold mine, bank, church.\end{DoxyVerb}
\begin{DoxyVerb}Each player building is described by its front color, name, cost (number of food, wood, stone or of any type),
number of prestige points, [optionally] construction (actions phase), primary and secondary effect (effect phase).
\end{DoxyVerb}
